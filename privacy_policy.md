# ENGLISH

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MonsTD ("the Game", "we", "us") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

**We do not collect personal data directly.**

However, the Game uses third-party services that may process certain information:

### Apple Services
- **Game Center** (leaderboards, achievements): Managed entirely by Apple and subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy.
- **iCloud** (game progress sync): Your data is stored via Apple’s iCloud services. We do not have access to this data.
- **In-App Purchases** (StoreKit): Payments are processed entirely by Apple. We do not store or access payment information.

### Advertising
- **Google AdMob**: The Game displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information such as device identifiers (IDFA) and usage data to provide personalised advertising.
  - This occurs only after you grant permission via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt.
  - If permission is denied, only non-personalised ads will be shown.

### Analytics
- The Game stores anonymous crash data locally on your device for diagnostic purposes. This data is not transmitted externally.

---

## 3. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission to track activity via the ATT prompt.

You can change this preference at any time under:
Settings → Privacy → Tracking

Denying permission does not affect gameplay.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

## 5. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers.

Game progress is stored:
- Locally on your device  
- Optionally via iCloud (managed by Apple)
